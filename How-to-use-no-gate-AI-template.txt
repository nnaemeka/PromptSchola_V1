How to use this free template

Save as nano-template-free.html.

Duplicate for a new lesson, e.g. nano-what-is-physics.html.

Update:

  * <title>, description, hero title/subtitle, breadcrumbs.

  * NANO_SLUG (e.g. what_is_physics).

  * Step titles/body text.

  * p,rompts[1..6] to match that topic.
