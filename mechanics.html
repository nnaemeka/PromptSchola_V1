Mechanics

Forces, motion, and energy: the backbone of Physics.

Mechanics is where most Physics journeys truly begin. Here you’ll learn how and why objects move, how forces act, and how energy and momentum shape the world.

In the Mechanics track, every lesson follows the same 6-step flow:

  • 1. Orient – get the overview and key questions.
  • 2. Understand – build intuition with clear explanations.
  • 3. Apply – connect ideas to real systems.
  • 4. Work Through It – step-by-step examples.
  • 5. Review & Practice – questions that reveal gaps.
  • 6. Simulation Lab – visualize how changing variables affects motion.

Start wherever you are: first-time learner, revisiting for an exam, or using Mechanics as a foundation for engineering and advanced Physics.

Steps 1–3 are free for every Mechanics lesson. Steps 4–6 unlock with Mastery Access.
