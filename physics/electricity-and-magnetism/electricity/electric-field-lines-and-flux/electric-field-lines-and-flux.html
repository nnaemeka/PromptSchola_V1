Electricity · Physics

Field lines help you see E. Flux helps you measure it.

Field lines are a visual representation of a vector field. Electric flux converts “field through a surface” into a number, preparing you for Gauss’s law.

Electric Field Lines and Flux

Learn the rules for drawing field lines, then compute flux through flat and curved surfaces.

Visualization
Field line representation
Field lines are a drawing tool: the tangent to a line gives the field direction, and arrows show the direction a positive test charge would move.
  • Lines point from + to − (by convention)
  • Tangent gives direction of E
  • Lines never cross
  • Lines are not trajectories
