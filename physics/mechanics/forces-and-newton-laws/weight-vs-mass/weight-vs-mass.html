Weight vs Mass

Mass is an intrinsic property. Weight is a force that depends on gravity and motion.

This topic includes

Subtopics to master

Get the definitions right, then master apparent weight and “scale reading” problems.

Definition
Definition of Mass
Mass measures inertia and sets how much acceleration results from a given net force.
  • Inertial vs gravitational mass
  • Units (kg)
  • Constant across locations
Start subtopicMass ≠ weight.
Definition
Definition of Weight
Weight is the gravitational force on an object (near Earth often W = mg).
  • Force units (N)
  • Direction: toward Earth’s center
  • Varies with g

Weight as a Force
Weight belongs on FBDs. Mass belongs in ΣF = ma.

  • Don’t put “m” as a force
  • Common representation errors
