About What PromptSchola is building

Prompt-guided STEM mastery, step by step.

PromptSchola turns STEM topics into a structured learning flow so students always know what to ask, what to practice, and how to move from “I kinda get it” to real mastery.

Why PromptSchola exists

Students often have access to AI, notes, and videos — but still feel stuck because they don’t know the right next question to ask, or how to build confidence across tricky variations. PromptSchola solves that with a guided, repeatable learning system.

How it works

Each topic is broken into nano-lessons with a consistent flow: you build intuition, practice intentionally, and learn how to avoid common mistakes — with AI support when appropriate.

  • Clarity first: short explanations, clean examples, no fluff.
  • Practice with purpose: questions are designed to surface common misconceptions.
  • AI as a coach: guided prompts help students reason, not just copy answers.
  • Exam readiness: common traps, sign errors, graph misreads, and “top of motion” moments.
